Iced Matcha Latte

Iced Matcha Latte - The Drink of the GODS

If you want to feel like Zeus, and have the power of lightning within your hands. Then MAKE THIS DRINK !!!!!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1-2 Tsp Matcha
  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla Syrup
  • 1 cup ice

Steps:

  1. Place milk, matcha powder, and vanilla syrup together in a cup.
  2. Mix well with a regular spoon or a milk frother.
  3. Once well combined, add ice.
